How To Play

Objective: Position 3 of your pieces in a row anywhere on the three-dimensional 3x3x3 game board. Achieve this run vertically, horizontally, diagonally, or straight through the middle. Players alternate turns placing their pieces until one achieves a run of 3 or the board fills up without a winner. If the board fills up, the second player wins the game.

The Twist: You can either place a new piece in an empty square, or push existing pieces in your chosen direction, provided the push doesn't force pieces out of the cube. You cannot place a piece in the middle directly, and must start from one of the outer squares instead. Rotate the board using your arrow keys or W, A, S, D to face any direction. Then, place your piece on the side facing you, which pushes any piece in that square towards the back of the board.

Neutral Pieces: Fifteen "neutral" black pieces start on the board as obstacles, placed in a random pattern generated each game you play. Use these to block your opponent or secure advantageous positions for your pieces. With only 12 open squares available, filling the board without completing a run of three is a real possibility. This dynamic creates a complex set of strategies, making this simple looking game easy to learn but challenging to master.

Hope you enjoy!
